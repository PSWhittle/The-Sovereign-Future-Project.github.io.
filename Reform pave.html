About the Campaign

Our campaign is based on a firm, lawful strategy to address the migration crisis, reduce illegal crossings, and uphold British values and laws.

Our 7-Pillar Strategy

  • Secure Detention & Offshore Processing
  • Legal Inadmissibility for Illegal Entrants
  • Legal Reform & UK Sovereignty
  • Deportation & Reintegration Treaties
  • Domestic Enforcement of Immigration Laws
  • Citizenship & Welfare Reform
  • Public Campaign & National Messaging
